## Major Cities (for sub-regions)

### Cairo & Giza Areas
- **Cairo Governorate**: Downtown, Nasr City (مدينة نصر), Heliopolis (مصر الجديدة), Maadi (المعادي), Zamalek (الزمالك), Mokattam (المقطم), 15th of May City, Shubra (شبرا)
- **Giza Governorate**: Dokki (الدقي), Mohandeseen (المهندسين), Haram (الهرم), 6th of October City (السادس من أكتوبر), Sheikh Zayed (الشيخ زايد), Agouza (العجوزة), Imbaba (إمبابة)
- **Qalyubia (touches Cairo)**: Shubra El Kheima (شبرا الخيمة), Banha (بنها), Qalyub (قليوب)

### Alexandria Area
- **Alexandria City Districts**: El Raml, Moharram Bek, Sporting, Sidi Bishr, Mandara, Montazah, Smouha, Miami, Al Agami, King Marriott, Stanley, San Stefano

### Canal Cities
- **Port Said**: Sharq, Gharb, Jawad Hosni
- **Ismailia**: First District, Second District, Third District
- **Suez**: Suez City, Port Tawfik, Al-Arbaeen

- **Kafr El Sheikh** (capital of Kafr El Sheikh)

## Notes
- Population figures are estimates for reference only.
- Cities can be added as sub-regions under their respective governorate.
- Some areas overlap jurisdiction (e.g., new urban communities may be under multiple governorates).
- The admin can add any custom region (e.g., "New Cairo") — this file is only a reference.
- For country-wide or nationwide delivery, consider adding a "Nationwide" catch-all region.
